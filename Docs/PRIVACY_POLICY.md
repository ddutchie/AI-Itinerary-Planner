# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 04 September 2025  
**Last Updated:** 04 September 2025

## Introduction

AI Travel Itinerary Planner ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

**Travel Preferences:**
- Destination selections and travel dates
- Budget preferences (budget, mid-range, luxury)
- Activity preferences and interests
- Accommodation preferences and locations
- Special places you want to visit
- Custom notes and trip details

**Account Information:**
- Premium subscription status and purchase history
- App usage preferences and settings
- Theme and display preferences

### Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information:**
- Device type, operating system, and version
- App version and usage analytics
- Crash reports and performance data

**Location Information:**
- Location data when you search for destinations
- Geocoding information for trip planning
- Map interactions and location preferences

**Usage Data:**
- Features used within the App
- Time spent using different functions
- Trip generation frequency and patterns

### Information from Third-Party Services

**Google Services:**
- Google Places API data for location search and details
- Google Maps data for location visualization
- Place details including ratings, photos, and reviews

**AI Processing Services:**
- Travel preferences sent to AI services for itinerary generation
- Premium users: Enhanced processing through OpenAI GPT-4o
- Free users: Standard AI processing services

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

**Core App Functions:**
- Generate personalized travel itineraries
- Provide location-based recommendations
- Display maps and location details
- Process premium subscriptions and purchases

**Service Improvement:**
- Analyze app usage to improve features
- Optimize itinerary generation algorithms
- Enhance user experience and interface
- Debug and fix technical issues

**Communication:**
- Send important app updates
- Provide customer support
- Notify about new features or changes

## Data Storage and Security

**Local Storage:**
- Trip data is primarily stored locally on your device
- Preferences and settings stored in device storage
- Offline functionality for previously generated itineraries

**Security Measures:**
- Industry-standard encryption for data transmission
- Secure connections (HTTPS) for all external communications
- Regular security updates and monitoring
- Limited data retention policies

**Data Retention:**
- Trip data: Retained until you delete it
- Usage analytics: Retained for 24 months
- Crash reports: Retained for 12 months
- Purchase records: Retained as required by law

## Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

**Access and Control:**
- View and manage your trip data within the App
- Delete trips and personal information at any time
- Modify preferences and settings
- Export your trip data

**Data Deletion:**
